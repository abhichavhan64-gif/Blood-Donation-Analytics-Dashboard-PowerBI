## 📌 Project Overview

The **Blood Donation Analytics Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI report designed to analyze blood donor data and uncover meaningful insights related to donor demographics, blood groups, donation frequency, active donors, city-wise donor distribution, and monthly donation trends.

This dashboard helps blood banks and healthcare organizations monitor donor activity, identify high-demand blood groups, understand repeat donor behavior, and make data-driven decisions to improve blood donation campaigns.

> 💡 **Goal:** Transform raw blood donation data into useful insights that can support healthcare decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- 🩸 Analyze total donors and total blood donations.
- 🧬 Identify the most common blood groups among donors.
- 👥 Compare donor distribution by gender and age group.
- 🏙️ Identify cities with the highest number of blood donors.
- 📅 Track monthly donation activity and donor trends.
- 🔁 Analyze first-time and repeat donor frequency.
- 🎛️ Enable dynamic filtering using slicers for deeper analysis.

## 📈 Dashboard Visualizations

### 1️⃣ Donors by Blood Group

Displays the number of donors for each blood group including O+, B+, A+, O-, AB+, A-, B-, and AB-.

This visualization helps identify the most available and least available blood groups.

### 2️⃣ Donors by Gender

A pie chart displaying donor distribution across:

- 👨 Male
- 👩 Female
- 🧑 Other

This helps compare donor participation based on gender.

### 3️⃣ Donors by Age Group

A bar chart showing donor count across age groups:

- 18–25
- 26–35
- 36–45
- 45+

This helps identify the age group with the highest blood donation participation.

### 4️⃣ Top 10 Cities by Donors

A horizontal bar chart displaying cities with the highest number of donors.

This helps identify cities where blood donation awareness and participation are stronger.

### 5️⃣ Monthly Donation Trend

A line chart displaying donor activity month-wise.

This helps identify months with high and low donor participation.

### 6️⃣ Donation Frequency

A donut chart showing donor distribution based on donation frequency:

- 🆕 First Time Donors
- 🔁 2–3 Times Donors
- 🔄 4–5 Times Donors
- ⭐ 5+ Times Donors

This visualization helps understand repeat donor engagement.

## 🔍 Key Insights

- 🩸 **O+ blood group** has the highest number of donors.
- 🎂 The **26–35 age group** has the highest blood donation participation.
- 👥 Male and female donor participation is almost equal.
- 🏙️ Mohali, Kanpur, Chennai, Surat, and Ahmedabad are among the top donor cities.
- 🔁 Around **63.41%** of donors are repeat donors.
- ❤️ Blood donation activity has impacted approximately **63K lives**.
- 🌍 The dashboard analyzes donor data across **20 cities**.
- 📈 Each donor has contributed an average of **2.21 donations**.
